XROS


Motivation

Virtual Reality (VR) has been introduced for some time but sadly, current technology is not advanced enough to make the scenes immersive enough. Our team wants to build fundamental functions and UI structures to better utilize 3D space and eliminate effect that may cause vertigo.


  • Adjustable Pointer

    You are now able to change direction of emitted ray from controllers. Players are now more relax to enjoy VR!

  • 3D Input (Punch Typing)

    You may have your personalized way to type in VR. Try arrange the keyboard by yourself!

  • 3D Folder

    It is hard to localize 3D in VR? The depth matters!

  • Virtual Equipment

    Not only controllers -- headphone, ID card, tool bar and more !

  • Human Skeleton Calibration

    Items too far? World too big? Calibrate the world to you first!

  • Reposition UIs

    The center of VR world is you! Everything should be there at your convenience.
